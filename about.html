Who We Are

KenSports TV is Uganda's number one free sports streaming platform, built with passion for Ugandan sports fans. We believe every sports fan in Uganda deserves access to live sports without paying expensive subscription fees.

Our platform brings you live football, cricket, rugby, boxing, basketball, motorsport and much more — all completely free, no registration required.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple — to make live sports accessible to every Ugandan, regardless of their financial situation. We believe sports unites people, and we want to be the platform that brings Ugandans together to enjoy the sports they love.

From the Uganda Premier League to the English Premier League, from IPL Cricket to NBA Basketball — KenSports TV has it all for free.
