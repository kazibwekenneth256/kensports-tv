Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is KenSports TV completely free?
Yes! KenSports TV is 100% free. No subscription, no registration, no payment required.


Q: Why is a stream not working?
Streams can go offline due to server issues. Try clicking another server button or check back later. We update streams regularly.


Q: Can I request a channel or match?
Yes! Use the contact form above and select "Request a channel/match" to make your request.


Q: How do I install KenSports TV on my phone?
Open KenSports TV in your browser → tap the menu → select "Add to Home Screen". It works like a real app!


Q: Is KenSports TV available on Smart TV?
Yes! Open the KenSports TV link in your Smart TV browser and it works perfectly on big screens.
