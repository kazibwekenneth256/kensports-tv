Introduction

Welcome to KenSports TV — Uganda's #1 free sports streaming platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience on our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use KenSports TV.

Information We Collect

KenSports TV may collect the following types of information:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, PC, TV)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the platform
  • General location data (country/region level only)
  • IP address for security purposes

We do NOT collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Improve and optimize the KenSports TV platform
  • Understand which sports content is most popular
  • Ensure the security and stability of our service
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns

Advertising

KenSports TV uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to show ads based on your interests and previous visits to our website.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com.

Cookies

KenSports TV uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Keep you connected to our service
  • Analyze site traffic via Google Analytics
  • Deliver relevant advertisements

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of KenSports TV.

Streaming Content

KenSports TV does NOT host any video content on its servers. All streams are links to publicly available content on the internet.

We are not responsible for the content of third-party streams. KenSports TV acts as a directory/aggregator of publicly available sports streams.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

KenSports TV does not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent.

Children's Privacy

KenSports TV is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

Continued use of KenSports TV after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
